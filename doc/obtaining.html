Obtaining fancylogin

fancylogin is available in the following formats:

  • Standard gzip compressed tarballs. They can be downloaded from:

    • http://fancylogin.sourceforge.net/

    • ftp://fancylogin.sourceforge.net/pub/fancylogin/

    • ftp://sunsite.unc.edu/pub/linux/system/admin/login/ and every other sunsite mirror.

  • RPM packages. These packages can be obtained from:

    • http://fancylogin.sourceforge.net/

  • Debian packages. Download them from:

    • http://www.debian.org/Packages/unstable/admin/fancylogin.html
